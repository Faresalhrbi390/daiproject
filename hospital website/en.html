Pre-diabetic stage

What is it

It is a condition that causes sugar to rise to higher than normal blood levels, but does not rise to the level of type 2 diabetes, but leaving the condition without making lifestyle changes makes adults and children who have a pre-diabetic stage at a greater risk of developing type 2 diabetes.

prevention

Starting a healthy life, avoiding bad habits, and exercising regularly

symptoms

Feeling more thirsty than usual. Unintentional weight loss. Presence of ketones in the urine. Ketones are a by-product of the breakdown of muscle and fat that occurs when there is not enough insulin in the body.

treatment

Losing weight, eating healthy foods, and exercising can help control diabetes. If a healthy diet and exercise don't keep your blood sugar under control, you may also need diabetes medications or insulin therapy.

Types of diabetes

first type

It is the complete lack of insulin secretion due to damage to the beta cells in the pancreas, which makes the patient need to obtain insulin from an external source for life, and at this stage insulin needles are started.

second type

chronic disease that occurs when the body cannot produce enough insulin or use it effectively. People with type 2 diabetes need treatment to keep insulin and blood sugar levels under control.
